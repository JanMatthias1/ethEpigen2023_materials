Here I compared all DMR regions on chromosome 22 to all the genes on the chromosome. We can see that the biological regulation, regulation of biological process and regulation of cellular processes have a lower hyper fold enrichment but have more foreground regions and are highly significant. These functions makse sense, as chromosome 22 is often involved in cancer (biological regulation problem, cell cycle,). A possible explanation for this, is that as these regions are methylated (if inhibitory methylation), they can not properly regulate the functions.
